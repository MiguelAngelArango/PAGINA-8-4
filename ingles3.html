It spans cultures and faiths and is a universal age old concept which can’t fail! PAST SIMPLE: I DIDN’T LIKE WHEN PEOPLE TREATED ME BADLY PRESENT SIMPLE : I TREAT ALL PEOPLE RESPECTFULLY

Only then will you be able to understand each other and build bridges.
